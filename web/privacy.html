Ya Hala Arab Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as name, email address, phone number, profile photo, sign-in provider, and notification token.
  • Content you choose to publish, including ads, questions, coupons, photos, cities, addresses, prices, and contact methods.
  • Messages, comments, likes, favorites, views, search activity, and product interaction data used to operate and improve the app.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage accounts, publish and review listings, operate chat, notifications, coupons, favorites, and support.
  • To improve safety, prevent fraud or abuse, moderate user content, and understand app performance.
  • To show public listing details that users intentionally submit as part of an ad, coupon, question, or profile.

Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell personal data. Ya Hala Arab uses services such as Firebase, Google, Apple, cloud storage, notifications, and analytics. These providers may process information according to their own policies.

When you tap call, SMS, maps, or external links, your device or third-party apps may handle that action.

User Content and Public Listings

Listings, coupons, questions, comments, business information, addresses, prices, and photos may be visible to other users when you submit them publicly. Do not post sensitive personal information.

Security and Retention

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information, but no system is completely secure. We keep information as needed to operate the app, provide safety and support, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Your Choices

You may update your profile, edit or delete your listings where available, control notification permissions from your device settings, and request account deletion from the profile page inside the app.
